Mace subclass (melee)
- Subclass of melee swords
  - Animation like sword but returning back to the original position
- Has effect on hit on ground (horisontal position)
- And can use effect on hit (by projectile)
* Idea by WitherTaco

Paladin class
- He is subclass of Melee (can use Melee class accesories and buffs)
- He uses Mace
- Can add buffs for you team (like paladin`s shield accessory)
* Idea by tryreo and upgraded by WitherTaco
